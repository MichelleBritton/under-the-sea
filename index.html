Under the Sea

Welcome to Under the Sea Memory Game!

This is a two player game to test your memory skills.

The aim of the game is to match as many pairs of cards as possible. The person with the most matches wins. Each player takes alternate turns by clicking on two cards to see if they match.

To start, enter each Player's name into the boxes below and click on the Start button.
